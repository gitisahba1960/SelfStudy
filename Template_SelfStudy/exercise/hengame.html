She was born in Mashad, Iran in 1970. She graduated in BA of Human-Economic Geography from Islamic Azad University in Mashad and Shahre-Rey branches. She continued her education in western philosophy in San Francisco University. She has started her artistic career since 2000 with her first appearance in Twilight directed by Hassan Hedayt. Her first role in theatre was named Like blood for steak which was produced by Leev theatre group. For her activities in theatre, she has been candidate for National community for Iranian theatre in 2005 in respect to her role in the notebook written by Jan Clude Career and directed by Katayoun Feiz Marandi. In addition to her artistic activities, she has translated the book of Red man White man law, written by Wilkomb Vashborn, a story of the American Indians
